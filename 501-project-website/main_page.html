Data Science Data science is a set of specific methods to fit the real world with our existing patterns, to gain further understanding. We first “transform” reality to data, then find the underlying rules or relationships among them with mathematical methods and computers. In most cases, we apply these methods to solve problems in other fields, so a data science problem always seems like an interdisciplinary issue. Key Difference Firstly, a data science story is still a story whose mission is to tell us what has happened, while a technical report should provide a technical problem with a solution or an inspiring method unless it is published in a suspicious journal. Secondly, in the telling of a story, the structure, the sentences, the descriptive way, etc have to be attractive to gain more popularity and more readers (so that news agency can eventually make more money and we can eventually gain more points). However, a technical report can use as many esoteric and boring formulas as possible as long as it can contribute to the technological advancement. Lastly, no matter how objective you want to be, a story is always mixed with your subjective opinion(for example, the way you organize your story) due to the human nature. In contrast, a technical report is composed of rigorous inference and deduction, which is repeatable and resonable. Visual storytelling is important because it reduce the cost of understanding. A vivid example is: In China, a financial news app called WallstreetCN beats its competitors by providing several charts which reflects the stock market movement everyday.
